Question: Ingestion Strategy

How should the embedder.py handle updates to the knowledge base?

A

Wipe & Re-index (Simpler)

Clear the collection and re-embed everything. Best for initial development and medium datasets.

B

Incremental (Hash-based)

Check SHA-256 hashes of chunks/files. Only update what has changed. Prevents duplicate work.
