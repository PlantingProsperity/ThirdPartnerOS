## 2. PROCEDURAL HEURISTICS (Logic & Algorithms)

### Rule: The Deputizing Protocol
*   **Trigger:** Daily interaction with people in the marketplace.
*   **Action:** Explicitly state desire and reward.
*   **The Script:** "Here’s what I want. Here’s what I’m looking for. Keep your eyes peeled for me and if you turn me on to something, I will pay you well for it."
*   **Why:** To multiply eyes on the street; the augers must run constantly.

### Rule: The Three-Minute Go/No-Go
*   **Trigger:** Assessing a potential real estate deal.
*   **Action:** Immediate decision making (approx. 3 minutes).
*   **The Math:** Pre-loaded knowledge of [Cost of Construction] + [Cost of Development] + [Market Rents] + [Market Value].
*   **Why:** Power Players do not "guess" or spend months analyzing. They study values daily so execution is instant.

### Rule: The Empty-Handed Law
*   **Trigger:** Moving from Point A to Point B (e.g., Upstairs to Garage).
*   **Action:** Scan environment for items that need to be transported to the destination or points en route.
*   **Why:** Extreme efficiency. Never walk anywhere empty-handed. "Get six things done en route."

### Rule: The Book Report Discipline
*   **Trigger:** Reading a biography or educational book.
*   **Action:** Write a full synopsis/book report outlining key principles of success derived from the text.
*   **Why:** Active retention vs. passive consumption.

## 3. MENTAL MODELS (Philosophy)

*   **Principle:** **"Put On Your Own Mask First"**
    *   **Mindset:** Life is an emergency. You cannot be powerful for others (family, partners) if you are depleted.
    *   **Application:** Establish a non-negotiable personal ritual (e.g., morning walk, hunting for golf balls) that centers your power before engaging with the world.

*   **Principle:** **"Miss The Toilet" (Re-framing Failure)**
    *   **Mindset:** "If you missed the toilet, you missed the toilet. Aim better from here on."
    *   **Application:** Do not spend energy reliving mistakes or asking "why." Analyze, adjust aim, and move on immediately.

*   **Principle:** **The Apollo 13 Effect**
    *   **Mindset:** When a project goes wrong/over budget, do not view it as a disaster. View it as a privilege and "our finest hour."
    *   **Application:** Reframing high-stress situations as the elite territory where few people ever get to test themselves.

*   **Principle:** **Bold vs. Arrogant**
    *   **Mindset:** Arrogance is assuming you are better; Boldness is knowing what you want and asking for it.
    *   **Application:** Direct, clear communication of desires without apology.

## 5. CASE STUDY DATABASE (Structured Data)

*   **Case Name:** The Great Eight (Composite Profile)
*   **Situation:** High-performing students selected for the "Power Players" week.
*   **Strategy Used:**
    *   Zero tolerance for negative people (Sissy Principle).
    *   Daily study of market values (rents/dirt).
    *   "No bad habits" (no smoking/excessive drinking).
    *   Treating the sheet rocker with the same attention as the Bank CEO.
*   **Outcome:** Acquired "hundreds and hundreds" of units; consistently high results in health and wealth.
*   **Key Lesson:** Success is less about what you do, and more about what you *don't* do (removing drag/negative inputs).

*   **Situation:** Greg as a 5-year-old needing to get milk from the garage.
*   **Strategy Used:** The "Empty Handed" rule. Checking for laundry to go down, or items to come up.
*   **Outcome:** Multiple tasks accomplished in a single transit.
*   **Key Lesson:** Efficiency adds energy; it does not drain it.
