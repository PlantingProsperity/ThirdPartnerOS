## 2. PROCEDURAL HEURISTICS (Logic & Algorithms)

### Rule: The Lateral Move Protocol
* **Trigger:** IF the direct path forward is blocked OR the "clear picture" of the destination is undefined.
* **Action:** THEN choose to make "no decision" on direction, hold the line of scrimmage, and move laterally (probing left and right) with full energy.
* **Why:** Moving forward without an opening wastes energy and resources. Moving laterally preserves resources for when the "Hole" opens.

### Rule: The 17-Second Impression
* **Trigger:** IF a buyer enters a property.
* **Action:** THEN they will form a predominant image/opinion within **17 seconds**.
* **The Math:** `Sale Success = (Sight + Smell + Sound + Touch) > Negative Bias`
* **Why:** Buyers make decisions based on emotion and sensory input (sniffing the air, feeling temperature) before logic engages.

### Rule: The Four Variables of Value
* **Trigger:** IF a seller wants "Top Dollar" (Maximum Price).
* **Action:** THEN they must optimize the other three variables: **Terms**, **Presentation**, and **The Team** (Agent Ability).
* **The Math:** `Price = Function(Terms + Presentation + Team)`
* **Why:** If a seller compromises on the Team (hiring a discount broker) or Presentation, the Price variable must drop to compensate.

### Rule: The Discount Broker Rejection
* **Trigger:** IF a seller asks, "Will you list this for a flat fee/discount because I can do the marketing myself?"
* **Action:** THEN reply, "I don't think I can help you."
* **Why:** A client who believes they are the expert leaves no room for the broker's expertise to create value.

## 3. MENTAL MODELS (Philosophy)

* **Principle:** **The Contrarian Mindset**
* **Mindset:** "If you want to have a different occurrence of life, it all starts with a mindset... If you want to be successful, you don't follow the herds."
* **Application:** When the market shifts and agents quit, you double down. When "old timers" say nobody knocks on doors, you knock on doors.

* **Principle:** **Illusion of Security**
* **Mindset:** Job security is an illusion. Working for a large corporation (like Boeing) carries as much risk as entrepreneurialism but with capped upside.
* **Application:** Control your own destiny by owning the business or the real estate.

* **Principle:** **Assumption is the Enemy**
* **Mindset:** Never assume a contract is correct or a buyer is qualified just because another agent says so.
* **Application:** Verify every "moving part" (lender, escrow, qualifications) personally.

## 5. CASE STUDY DATABASE (Structured Data)

* **Case Name:** The Staged Condo Experiment
* **Situation:** Greg built a 12-unit condo complex; sales were sluggish.
* **Strategy Used:** **Presentation/Staging**. The listing agent insisted on fully staging one unit (furniture, smells, music).
* **Outcome:** The staged unit sold immediately. Greg moved the exact same furniture to the next unit (even if it was a worse unit), and that one sold next. This repeated 11 times.
* **Key Lesson:** Buyers lack imagination. They buy the feeling of the "home," not the box. Presentation overrides location flaws.

* **Case Name:** The $70k Feuding Neighbor
* **Situation:** Lee Gallaher was door-knocking. Found a neighbor who wanted to move because he was feuding with the person next door.
* **Strategy Used:** **Lateral Questions / Integrity**. The seller said, "If someone gave me $70,000 I'd sell in a heartbeat."
* **Outcome:** Lee told him it was worth more (Competence/Integrity), but the seller insisted on $70k for speed/ease. Lee bought it for $70k.
* **Key Lesson:** Asking lateral questions creates inventory. Sometimes sellers value "exit" over "top dollar."

* **Case Name:** The First Door (Roy Hoglund)
* **Situation:** Rookie Lee Gallaher was told by veterans that "nobody knocks on doors because people are lazy."
* **Strategy Used:** **Reach Returns**. He went to 81st Place and knocked on the first door.
* **Outcome:** Roy Hoglund opened the door; Lee listed and sold the house.
* **Key Lesson:** The "herd" is often wrong. High-contact activity (Reach) generates immediate Returns.
