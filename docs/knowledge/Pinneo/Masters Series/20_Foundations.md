## 1. THE PINNEO DICTIONARY (Domain Lexicon)

| Term | Definition (In Pinneo Context) | Related Concept |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Reset** | A deliberate physiological and psychological process to restart momentum when life requires a change. Originally 4 steps, amended to 5. | Personal Philosophy |
| **Get Dead** | Step 3 of The Reset. The act of deliberately "unpacking" failure/baggage, setting it down, and letting go of it completely before moving forward. | Emotional Intelligence |
| **The Waiting Place** | A paralyzed state of existence where one waits for external conditions (time, money, permission) to be perfect before acting. Derived from Dr. Seuss. | Opportunity Cost |
| **Earth Suit** | A metaphor for the physical human body. | Mortality |

| **Deferred Interest Note** | A promissory note where the principal is fixed ("frozen"), and interest does not accrue or become payable until a triggering event (e.g., seller vacates). | Creative Financing |
| **Substitution of Security** | A clause allowing the borrower to move the collateral of a loan from the subject property to a different property of equal/greater value. | Asset Protection / Exit Strategy |
| **Work Orders** | Deferred maintenance items (roof, paint, electrical) used as line-item deductions to negotiate the purchase price down. | Negotiation |

## 2. PROCEDURAL HEURISTICS (Logic & Algorithms)

### Rule: The 5-Step Reset
* **Trigger:** IF [Momentum is lost, health fails, or a "rhythm" problem occurs in business/life]
* **Action:** THEN [Execute the 5 steps in order]
    1.  **Go In:** Act of humility; admit you don't have the answers.
    2.  **Go to Sleep:** Relax; stop grinding. Let the world function without you.
    3.  **Get Dead:** Unpack the baggage/failure and leave it there.
    4.  **Add Encouragement:** (The Critical Amendment) Inject an outside agent/mentor to help the heart surrender to change.
    5.  **Hit the Paddles:** Take action; choose newness; get moving.
* **Why:** Willpower alone often fails at the "action" stage without the catalyst of encouragement.

### Rule: The Adjusted "No Conditions" Price (Elderly Seller)
* **Trigger:** IF [Calculating an offer for a seller who would otherwise list with an agent]
* **The Math:** $$Market Value - (Market Value \times 0.07 \text{ Commission}) - \text{Work Orders} = \text{Maximum Offer Price}$$
* **Action:** THEN [Present this lower number as a "Net" number to the seller, explaining they save the commission and repair hassle.]
* **Why:** Establishes a basis for the deal that protects the buyer's equity while solving the seller's liquidity problem.

### Rule: The "Free & Clear" Flip (Using Substitution)
* **Trigger:** IF [Subject property will not cash flow as a rental AND long-term financing terms are excellent]
* **Action:** THEN [Use "Substitution of Security" clause at closing or shortly after.]
    1.  Move the Note/Deed of Trust to a different long-term hold property you own.
    2.  Subject property becomes "Free and Clear."
    3.  Sell subject property for cash (selling the "dirt and bones").
    4.  Keep the favorable financing (the note) attached to the other property.
* **Why:** Never pay off excellent financing. Keep the debt, sell the asset.

## 3. MENTAL MODELS (Philosophy)

* **Principle:** **The Law of Displacement**
* **Mindset:** "We don't add things onto our life without displacing something else."
* **Application:** You do not "have" time; you choose how to allocate the 24 hours. To add a deal or a skill (like flying), you must explicitly remove non-productive hours (TV, over-sleeping).

* **Principle:** **Money Follows Product**
* **Mindset:** "Money is a commodity. It's a box of nails. We rent money."
* **Application:** Never say "I don't have money." If the product (the deal) is solid, you are 20 phone calls away from renting the money needed.

* **Principle:** **The Default is "No"**
* **Mindset:** "Do nothing and the answer is no. Say nothing and the answer is no. Ask nothing and the answer is no."
* **Application:** Proactivity is the only variable that changes the default setting of the universe.

## 5. CASE STUDY DATABASE (Structured Data)

* **Case Name:** The Pilot's License
* **Situation:** Greg claimed for years he "didn't have time" to learn to fly. Wife forced the issue by booking non-refundable classes.
* **Strategy Used:** **Time Displacement**. He had to carve out hours previously wasted to fit the new passion.
* **Outcome:** Achieved Private, Instrument, Multi-engine, and Float ratings + bought 2 hangars and a plane in 1 year.
* **Key Lesson:** We have time for anything we decide we want to do.

* **Case Name:** The "No Money" Rejection
* **Situation:** Student's seller-financing offer was rejected; seller wanted all cash. Student wanted to quit.
* **Strategy Used:** **Private Lending / Money Follows Product**. Greg instructed them to call 20 people.
* **Outcome:** Student raised 4x the amount needed in one day and closed the deal.
* **Key Lesson:** The identity of the beneficiary (lender) doesn't matter; only the terms do.

* **Case Name:** The Frozen Note / Elderly Seller
* **Situation:** Elderly seller ($400k value) wants to stay in home but fears maintenance/taxes.
* **Strategy Used:**
    1.  **Price Adjustment:** Paid $350k (deducted commission/repairs).
    2.  **Deferred Interest Note:** $10k down. $340k note @ 6%, but interest/payments frozen until move-out.
    3.  **Substitution:** Collateral moved to a different property.
* **Outcome:** Greg holds property for 4 years at a "cost" of only interest on the holding costs (~$2,400 total). Sells property for $540k.
* **Key Lesson:** Solve the seller's emotional need (security/staying put) to secure favorable financial terms for the buyer.
