## 2. PROCEDURAL HEURISTICS (Logic & Algorithms)

### Rule: The 8-Step Achievement Framework
*   **Trigger:** IF a specific high-value goal is identified.
*   **Action:** THEN execute the following sequence:
    1.  **See the End:** Create a vivid, 3D engineered drawing of the result.
    2.  **Believe/Declare:** Speak of the arrival in the past tense ("I have done this").
    3.  **Planning/Research:** Assess the gap between the "ladder top" and current position.
    4.  **Sacrifice:** Apply the *Sissy Principle* to displace old habits for new actions.
    5.  **Execution:** Discipline to follow the schedule regardless of feelings/weather.
    6.  **Adjustment:** Steer the moving car; modify the plan based on feedback.
    7.  **Evaluation:** Measure advances against the timeline.
    8.  **Celebration:** Savor the arrival.
*   **Why:** "Every great achievement starts with working backwards."

### Rule: The "Pilot Funding" Algorithm (Reverse Engineering Math)
*   **Trigger:** IF a goal has a high financial cost (e.g., Flight School = $30,000) and time cost (1,000 hours).
*   **Action:** THEN calculate the daily activity required to fund it via Real Estate.
*   **The Math:**
    1.  **Cost:** $30,000 / 24 months = $1,250/month needed.
    2.  **Revenue Target:** 1 Assignment Fee = $20,000. Need 2 Fees = $40,000 (covers $30k cost + $10k marketing).
    3.  **Marketing Budget:** $10,000 (funded via Unsecured Line of Credit).
    4.  **Letters Required:** $10,000 buys ~7,500 letters.
    5.  **Funnel Conversion:**
        *   7,500 Letters $\rightarrow$ 750 Calls.
        *   750 Calls $\rightarrow$ 375 "Real" Leads (remove 50% Looky-loos).
        *   375 Leads $\rightarrow$ 2 Deals.
    6.  **Daily Grind:** To send 7,500 letters, address 20-25 envelopes every morning (5:00 AM - 6:00 AM).
*   **Why:** Transforms a vague wish ("I want to fly") into a mechanical daily task ("Address 20 envelopes").

### Rule: The Commitment-Providence Loop
*   **Trigger:** IF you are waiting for "luck" or the "right time" to start.
*   **Action:** THEN you must commit first.
*   **Logic:** Providence (divine help) = 0 UNTIL Commitment = 1.
*   **Why:** "It doesn't get off the bench and in the game until you commit."

## 3. MENTAL MODELS (Philosophy)

*   **Principle:** **The Extinction of the Soul**
*   **Mindset:** Just as an animal dies without the correct environment (water, shelter), a human soul dies without an environment that feeds its passion.
*   **Application:** Perform a "Hard Audit." If your current job/life is depleting you, you are on the Endangered Species List. No one will save you. You must transplant yourself.

*   **Principle:** **Motion Precedes Steering**
*   **Mindset:** "You can't steer a parked car."
*   **Application:** Do not wait for a perfect plan. Start moving (Execution), then use "Adjustment" to fix the course. Rigid plans break; flexible plans in motion succeed.

*   **Principle:** **Displacement (The Physics of Time)**
*   **Mindset:** You cannot add to a 24-hour day. To add a new goal, you must displace an existing activity.
*   **Application:** Use the *Sissy Principle* to remove "squishers" and create a vacuum for the new endeavor.
