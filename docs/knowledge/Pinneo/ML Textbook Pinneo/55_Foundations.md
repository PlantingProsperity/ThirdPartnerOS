## 2. PROCEDURAL HEURISTICS (Logic & Algorithms)

### Rule: The Veto Card Protocol
* **Trigger:** IF the spouse (Shauna) feels a strong negative intuition or stress regarding a potential property/project.
* **Action:** THEN the deal is dropped immediately. No debate.
* **The Math:** N/A (Binary Decision: Yes/No)
* **Why:** "If mama ain't happy, ain't nobody happy." Proceeding against the spouse's gut creates an "uphill battle" that drains energy needed for success.

### Rule: The Courtesy Threshold ($5,000 Rule)
* **Trigger:** IF a purchase exceeds a set amount (e.g., $5,000).
* **Action:** THEN a discussion must occur between partners prior to execution.
* **The Math:** `If Cost > $5,000 = Discuss`
* **Why:** It is not about permission; it is about strategic awareness and courtesy. Below the threshold, autonomy is absolute.

### Rule: Domain Deference (The Role Reversal)
* **Trigger:** IF Partner A enters Partner B's "Wheelhouse" (e.g., Shauna on a construction site, Greg at home with kids).
* **Action:** THEN Partner A subordinates their opinion and works *for* Partner B.
* **Why:** Prevents "too many cooks" and respects the distinct expertise required for the specific environment.

## 3. MENTAL MODELS (Philosophy)

* **Principle:** **The Secret to Failure**
* **Mindset:** "The secret to failure is trying to please everybody." (Bill Cosby).
* **Application:** In business and content creation, do your best, but accept that some things are illogical and some people will never be pleased. Move on.

* **Principle:** **The Duck Blind (Work/Life Blur)**
* **Mindset:** For the self-unemployed, there is no "off" switch. A weekend is just another day to work if you let it.
* **Application:** You must aggressively self-discipline structure, otherwise business will bleed into leisure (like taking calls while duck hunting).

* **Principle:** **Ignorance is Not Bliss**
* **Mindset:** A pilot wants to know what the gauges are reading, even if they are bad.
* **Application:** Both partners must know the financial reality (even if "running on fumes") rather than hiding stress. Shared knowledge reduces anxiety.

* **Principle:** **The Cause**
* **Mindset:** "You haven't lived until you found something worth dying for."
* **Application:** Financial success is hollow without a philanthropic vision (e.g., housing single moms) to drive the work ethic.
