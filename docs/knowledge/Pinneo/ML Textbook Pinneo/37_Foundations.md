## 2. PROCEDURAL HEURISTICS (Logic & Algorithms)

### Rule: The Likelihood Filter
* **Trigger:** IF starting a prospecting session for income streams.
* **Action:** THEN do not call randomly. First, boil down the masses into a small number of "high likelihood" possibilities. Filter again to create "even higher likelihoods."
* **The Math:** `Efficiency = (Total Leads - Low Probability Leads) / Time`
* **Why:** To prevent being "taken out to sea on some random hunt" and ensure every step is a direct measure toward the objective.

### Rule: The Competent Pilot Validation
* **Trigger:** IF asking for money (from a bank or private party).
* **Action:** THEN you must present three non-negotiable elements (The Trifecta):
  1. **Confidence:** Exude that you know the flight plan.
  2. **Education:** Prove substance behind the confidence (answer detailed questions).
  3. **Collateral/Income:** Prove the project justifies the delivery of money.
* **Why:** "Nobody is going to board your aircraft if you don’t have credentials... or if you're not sure what button to push."

### Rule: The Lender Binder Strategy
* **Trigger:** IF presenting a deal to a banker or private lender.
* **Action:** THEN present a 5-inch thick, three-ring binder with the following specific tabs:
  1. **Cover:** Embossed with the Lender's Name (e.g., "Submittals for [Bank Name]").
  2. **Tab 1:** Table of Contents.
  3. **Tab 2:** Resume & Credit Report.
  4. **Tab 3:** 3 Years of Business Tax Returns + 3 Years of Personal Tax Returns.
  5. **Tab 4:** Schedule of Real Estate Owned (SREO) (with full-page color glossy photos of every building).
  6. **Tab 5:** The Proposition of the Day (Plans, specs, pro forma, numbers).
* **Why:** It creates an "Intellectual Yes" before the numbers are even read. It answers the question "Are you a competent pilot?" physically.

## 3. MENTAL MODELS (Philosophy)

* **Principle:** Time as an Equalizer
* **Mindset:** "Time is not a resource to be found; it is prioritized."
* **Application:** When tempted to say "I don't have time," look at a busy, successful person (like Jeff Surridge). They have the same 24 hours. The difference is they have eliminated the "SISSEY" items and linked every hour to a specific destination.

* **Principle:** The Bait Theory (Old Boot vs. Worms)
* **Mindset:** "Money will not be drawn to projects that don’t justify the delivery of money."
* **Application:** Do not troll with an "old boot" (a bad deal/poor presentation) and wonder why you catch no fish. You must use what the fish (investors) want: Security, Yield, and Competence.

* **Principle:** The Runway of Belief
* **Mindset:** "Opportunities are circling all the time. But they only land on a mind of belief."
* **Application:** If there is 1/100th of an ounce of doubt, the opportunity cannot land. You must believe to your "last breath" to clear the runway for the deal.
